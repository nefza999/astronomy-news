About Our Team

Meet the passionate minds behind Astronomy News - bridging astronomy with technology to bring the universe closer to everyone.

Mejri Ziad

Project Lead & Developer

Passionate astronomer and full-stack developer with over 5 years of experience in web development and 3 years in astronomy outreach. Created Astronomy News to make astronomy accessible to everyone, combining technical expertise with a love for celestial exploration. Leads the AstroMed Club and organizes stargazing events across Tunisia. Also runs a mystery/astronomy blog at Home of Mystery.

AstroMed Club

A multidisciplinary team of astronomy enthusiasts, developers, and science communicators dedicated to making space exploration accessible to all.

Our Mission

To democratize access to astronomical knowledge and space exploration updates through innovative digital platforms. We believe that understanding the universe should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background or location. By combining accurate scientific information with engaging digital experiences, we aim to inspire the next generation of astronomers, scientists, and space enthusiasts.
