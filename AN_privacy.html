Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Astronomy News. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our Progressive Web App (PWA), or interact with our services.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Create an account or register
  • Subscribe to notifications
  • Post comments or reactions
  • Contact us for support
  • Use social login (Google)

Automatically Collected Information

When you access our services, we automatically collect certain information including:

  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and general location
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • PWA installation status

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used Legal Basis
Provide and maintain our service Account information, device data Contractual necessity
Personalize user experience Preferences, usage patterns Legitimate interest
Send notifications and updates Email, notification preferences Consent
Improve our services Usage analytics, feedback Legitimate interest

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates for the purposes outlined above.

Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service, provide the service on our behalf, perform service-related services, or assist us in analyzing how our service is used.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal information where required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our security measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password storage using hashing
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section.

PWA-Specific Privacy Information

Our Progressive Web App (PWA) uses local storage and service workers to provide offline functionality. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly required.

Push Notifications: We request permission to send push notifications. You can disable notifications at any time through your browser or device settings.

Offline Data: Your interactions, comments, and reactions made while offline are stored locally and synchronized when you reconnect to the internet.

Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
